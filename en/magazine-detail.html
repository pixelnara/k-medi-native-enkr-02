Gangnam Dermatology Tour — Apgujeong to Cheongdam

KMEDITOUR Beauty Editor June 15, 2026 6 min read
SCROLL
Beauty Dermatology Gangnam Apgujeong Cheongdam Clinic Guide
Share

Within a roughly two-kilometre stretch between Apgujeong-dong and Cheongdam-dong in Gangnam, Seoul's most celebrated dermatology clinics stand in quiet concentration. Having personally accompanied hundreds of international clients over three years as a beauty coordinator, I have distilled the essentials into seven clinics and everything you should know before you arrive.

01

Why This Corridor

The Apgujeong–Cheongdam clinic belt is not simply renowned for its density of dermatologists. This district is where competition is at its most intense — and, accordingly, where standards of practice are at their highest. Korea's most accomplished dermatologists choose to open here, and it is invariably the first address to receive the latest generation of equipment.

There is also a thoroughly practical consideration. The surrounding infrastructure for recovery and leisure is exceptional. Apgujeong Rodeo, the Cheongdam luxury corridor, Galleria Department Store, and a constellation of refined cafés and restaurants are all within comfortable walking distance — creating an ideal environment for a composed afternoon following any treatment.

Apgujeong Rodeo. The heart of Seoul's beauty district, where clinics, cafés, and restaurants share a single, elegant neighbourhood.

"The clinics along this corridor compete in a way that quietly elevates the entire standard of beauty practice in Seoul."

KMEDITOUR Senior Coordinator
02

Before You Arrive

The single most important consideration is reservation. Walk-in availability at sought-after clinics is effectively non-existent, and requesting a specific specialist may require two to three weeks' notice. Through a KMEDITOUR coordinator, your preferred time can be secured before you even board your flight.

Interpretation is equally important to arrange in advance. While many leading clinics offer English-language support, nuanced consultations about your skin condition and post-treatment instructions are best handled by a dedicated professional interpreter — for both clarity and your peace of mind.

Pre-Visit Checklist

  • Reserve at least two weeks before arrival — popular specialists book out early
  • Avoid direct sun exposure and alcohol the day before certain treatments
  • Confirm contraindications specific to your chosen procedure in advance
  • Carry your passport and travel insurance documents — some clinics require them
  • Allow at least three hours of free movement in your schedule after any treatment
03

The Editor's Seven

These clinics have been selected based on direct observation with hundreds of clients — not by brand recognition or review volume alone, but through consistent outcomes, attentive aftercare, and the quality of their service for international guests.

Inside one of Gangnam's leading dermatology clinics — defined by the latest laser technology and an atmosphere of unhurried calm.

01 · Apgujeong Clinic A — The definitive address for lifting treatments. Among the earliest to integrate combination Ultherapy and Thermage protocols, with three board-certified dermatologists on staff. International concierge interpretation available.

02 · Cheongdam Clinic B — Unrivalled results in pigmentation and melasma correction. Renowned for its PicoLaser expertise, with a particularly high proportion of Asian international guests.

03 · Nonhyeon Clinic C — The filler and Botox specialist. Celebrated for its natural, considered results — the clinic we most frequently recommend to first-time visitors. Pre-treatment simulation service available.

04 · Apgujeong Clinic D — A pioneer in skin regeneration and stem-cell-based protocols. The address of choice for those seeking the most advanced regenerative care available in Seoul today.

05 · Cheongdam Clinic E — High-frequency lifting specialists. Among the first in Korea to adopt Thermage FLX next-generation technology, with exceptional treatment longevity.

06 · Apgujeong Clinic F — Dedicated to deep hydration and skin calming. Ideally visited as a conditioning course before or after more intensive laser treatments.

07 · Sinsa Clinic G — Exceptional value without compromise on results. Shorter wait times than its Apgujeong and Cheongdam counterparts, run by a young specialist with a keen sensitivity to emerging trends.

"Results are not determined by a clinic's name. They are determined by matching the right clinic and specialist to your unique skin and goals."

KMEDITOUR Beauty Editor
04

How to Spend the Rest of Your Day

The majority of treatments permit you to step outside immediately afterwards. You may notice some mild redness or a slight sensation of warmth — simply avoid direct sunlight and apply sunscreen with care. Here is a composed itinerary for the hours following your appointment.

A quiet lane in Cheongdam. Some of Seoul's finest restaurants are found here — a natural setting for an unhurried evening after your clinic visit.

Galleria Department Store's luxury wing is an ideal post-treatment destination — entirely indoors, and an excellent place to browse or pick up a cooling sheet mask to bring back to your hotel.

For something lighter, the organic cafés and avocado bowl establishments near Apgujeong Rodeo are a thoughtful choice. Antioxidant-rich green smoothies and acai bowls make a considered accompaniment to your skin's recovery process.

By evening, if you feel well rested, reserve a table in one of Cheongdam's intimate fine-dining rooms. From elevated Korean cuisine to modern French interpretations, the restaurants of this neighbourhood stand comfortably among the finest in any city.

05

With KMEDITOUR

Every clinic featured in this guide is a partner within the KMEDITOUR network. Your dedicated coordinator will begin by understanding your skin concerns and treatment goals before matching you with the most suitable clinic and specialist.

From reservation and interpretation to transportation and post-treatment care, every detail is managed on a one-to-one basis. Whether this is your first visit to Korea or a return journey, you are free to focus entirely on your experience — we take care of everything else.
