The Hugro Center Experience

AI Skin Analysis —
Where Your Skincare Begins

Our advanced AI skin analysis system provides a precise, data-driven assessment of your skin's condition — quantifying hydration, sebum, pore clarity, pigmentation, and elasticity to determine the care approach most suited to your complexion.

Rather than relying on questionnaires or visual inspection alone, the Hugro Center's approach begins with rigorous data. This commitment to precision is what distinguishes us.

Skin Analysis

Your AI analysis results are shared in real time with our specialist consultants, forming the foundation of a personalised care plan.
Each visit, your results are compared against previous analyses to track the evolution of your skin over time.
