Welcome to K-Medi Tour

K-Medi Tour is a premium concierge service designed to help international guests experience Korea's finest medical and beauty care — without language barriers, and with complete peace of mind. We work exclusively with rigorously vetted partner clinics across plastic surgery, dermatology, dentistry, traditional medicine, and health screening, maintaining privileged partnerships with Seoul's most distinguished medical institutions.

From your first inquiry through your safe return home, your dedicated coordinator is with you every step of the way. With multilingual support in English, Chinese, Japanese, and Vietnamese, and a track record of coverage in leading media, KMEDITOUR is the concierge partner you can trust for a journey that is entirely your own.

How It Works

01

Complimentary Consultation

Share your desired treatments, travel dates, and budget. Your dedicated coordinator will craft a bespoke plan encompassing medical, beauty, and travel arrangements — available in English, Chinese, Japanese, and Vietnamese.

02

Pre-Arrival Preparation

We handle clinic reservations, accommodation guidance, visa information, and itinerary planning — so your care begins well before you land in Korea. Leave every detail to us.

03

Full In-Person Accompaniment

From airport pickup to treatments, recovery, sightseeing, and departure, your coordinator accompanies you throughout. We also provide emergency support and post-treatment aftercare.

Our Promise to You

Verified Partner Clinics

We partner exclusively with Korea's most respected medical practitioners and clinics. Every partner is subject to rigorous specialist review and ongoing quality monitoring, ensuring an environment of clinical excellence.

Transparent Pricing

There are no hidden fees — ever. A detailed cost breakdown is provided before any treatment begins, and the final amount never exceeds what was agreed upon at the outset.

End-to-End Accountability

A single dedicated coordinator takes ownership of your entire journey — from initial consultation to departure. Reachable whenever you need us, your coordinator ensures you never feel alone in an unfamiliar city.
