Booking & Payment

Click "Book a Consultation" at the top of the page, or contact us via KakaoTalk at @KMEDITOUR. Once you share your desired services and travel dates, a dedicated coordinator will be assigned to craft your personalised plan.
Cancellations and modifications are accepted up to 72 hours prior to the scheduled treatment. Please notify us via KakaoTalk (@KMEDITOUR) or email (contact@kmeditour.com). Cancellations within 72 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee.
We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay), bank transfer, KakaoPay, and NaverPay. For international card payments, certain treatment packages may require advance remittance.
Full refunds are available for pre-treatment cancellations made at least 72 hours in advance. Cancellations on the day of treatment or no-shows may not be eligible for a refund. Cosmetics that are unopened and unused may be returned within 7 days of receipt.

Services

KMEDITOUR provides a full spectrum of premium concierge services for your visit to Korea: beauty treatments (skin procedures and aesthetics), travel and accommodation, cosmetic shopping, and visits to the Hugro Center.
Your 1-on-1 coordinator handles consultation bookings, clinic accompaniment, interpretation, post-treatment follow-up, and accommodation and sightseeing arrangements — offering close personal support throughout your entire stay. You can reach them at any time via KakaoTalk.
Our coordinators are fluent in English, Chinese, Japanese, and Vietnamese. If you would like a coordinator proficient in a specific language, simply let us know at the time of booking.
Our primary service area covers the Gangnam and Sinsa districts of Seoul. Jeju Island packages and select regional itineraries are available upon request — please contact us for a bespoke consultation.

Treatments

We connect guests with a wide range of aesthetic treatments including filler, Botox, thread lifting, laser, skin booster, skin care, and fat-dissolving procedures. All partner clinics maintain qualified medical teams and accredited facilities.
Share your skin concerns and desired outcomes via an online or in-person pre-consultation. Your coordinator will recommend appropriate treatments and clinics, and a medical consultation with the physician will take place at the clinic itself.
Your coordinator will check in within 48 hours of your treatment to monitor your recovery and provide care guidance. Online aftercare consultations are available even after you return home, and remote consultations with partner clinics can be arranged where needed.
Yes — international visitors may lawfully receive aesthetic treatments at Korean medical institutions. As an officially registered medical tourism agency, KMEDITOUR provides comprehensive support from visa guidance and documentation through to clinic bookings and accompaniment.

Cosmetic Shopping

You may order online through the Shopping section or the Cosmetics page of our website. In-store purchases are also available when visiting the Hugro Center.
Currently, we offer domestic delivery and in-person collection only. International shipping is in preparation and will be available in due course. Delivery to your accommodation in Korea during your stay is supported.
Unopened and unused products may be returned or exchanged within 7 days of receipt. In cases of adverse skin reactions or exceptional circumstances, please contact us at contact@kmeditour.com to discuss your situation.

General

The Hugro Center is located at 4F, 22 Apgujeong-ro 14-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul (Sinsa-dong) — approximately a 5-minute walk from Exit 2 of Apgujeong Station (Line 3). Appointments can be made via KakaoTalk or email.
We are available daily from 09:00 to 21:00 KST, 365 days a year. Please reach us via KakaoTalk (@KMEDITOUR) or email (contact@kmeditour.com). Enquiries received outside these hours will be responded to at the earliest opportunity.
KMEDITOUR complies fully with applicable personal data protection laws. Information collected is used solely for service delivery purposes. Any disclosure to third parties is subject to your prior consent, and all data is securely deleted upon conclusion of service. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for further details.
Please contact us via KakaoTalk at @KMEDITOUR or by email at contact@kmeditour.com. A dedicated consultant will respond promptly.
